# Product Strategy

## Product Thesis

The value of an internal AI knowledge assistant depends less on answer generation alone and more on workflow fit, user trust, human oversight, and measurable adoption.

## Target User

Primary users:

- Sales teams
- HR business partners
- Operations teams
- Customer support teams

## User Need

Users need fast, reliable answers to internal process and policy questions without wasting time searching across fragmented documentation.

## Adoption Barriers

Likely adoption barriers include:

- lack of trust in AI-generated answers
- concern about outdated sources
- unclear escalation paths
- fear of using AI incorrectly
- low-quality knowledge base content
- lack of integration into existing workflows

## Product Principles

1. Show the source whenever possible.
2. Make risk visible to the user.
3. Require human review for high-risk topics.
4. Capture feedback in the moment of use.
5. Measure adoption by behavior, not enthusiasm.
6. Scale only after repeat usage and trust are demonstrated.

## MVP Scope

The MVP includes:

- approved sample knowledge base
- question input
- answer display
- source citation
- risk label
- human-in-the-loop feedback
- adoption telemetry dashboard

## Out of Scope for v1

- full enterprise authentication
- production-grade retrieval
- integrations with Slack, Salesforce, or HRIS systems
- automated policy updates
- multi-agent workflows
- autonomous decision-making
