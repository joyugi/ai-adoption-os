# Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| User over-trusts AI answer | High | Medium | Show source, risk label, and escalation criteria |
| Source content is outdated | High | Medium | Assign content owners and review cadence |
| High-risk questions are answered without review | High | Medium | Require escalation for sensitive topics |
| Users do not adopt the tool | Medium | Medium | Start with high-frequency workflow and measure repeat usage |
| Feedback is not reviewed | Medium | Medium | Establish weekly feedback review |
| Business value is unclear | Medium | High | Track time saved, repeat usage, and workflow outcomes |
